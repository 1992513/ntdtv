<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>寒冬异象 河南电闪雷鸣下冰雹</h2>
<h6>2023年12月14日，河南洛阳市电闪雷鸣下冰雹。（网络视频截图）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年12月15日讯】正值寒冬季节，河南<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/洛阳市.md#1">洛阳市</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/电闪雷鸣.md#1">电闪雷鸣</a>，还降下<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/冰雹.md#1">冰雹</a>。同时，河南郑州和安阳等地也罕见地在冬日下冰雹。</p>
<p>北京时间12月14日早上6时许，有当地网友爆料，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/洛阳市.md#1">洛阳市</a>孟津区长时间<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/电闪雷鸣.md#1">电闪雷鸣</a>，雷声隆隆，伴随着火闪，照亮了半个天空。紧接着，就听见沙沙的冰粒<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/冰雹.md#1">冰雹</a>声，真乃奇景。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103834218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103834218" style="width: 360px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id103834218-GBS0Zf1aQAAyerT.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103834218" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id103834218-GBS0Zf1aQAAyerT-600x1333.jpg" alt="" width="360" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103834218" class="wp-caption-text">洛阳网友爆料，当地电闪雷鸣下冰雹。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当地传出的视频显示，居民从地上抓起一把黄豆大小的冰雹，并说：“这是冰雹，下冰雹了。”“冰雹的颗粒跟黄豆差不多大小吧。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_103834217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103834217" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id103834217-5320ed61-df40-404a-8d2e-1c3db1e1d5ab.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103834217" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id103834217-5320ed61-df40-404a-8d2e-1c3db1e1d5ab-600x388.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103834217" class="wp-caption-text">12月14日，河南洛阳市下冰雹。（网络视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103834210" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103834210" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id103834210-3cbb5451a5a837a38fb26259ca53efb9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103834210" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id103834210-3cbb5451a5a837a38fb26259ca53efb9.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="405" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103834210" class="wp-caption-text">12月14日，河南洛阳降下冰雹，约黄豆大小。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>公开资料显示，冰雹一般出现在较温暖的季节，主要出现在5月—9月，冬季极少降冰雹。寒冬电闪雷鸣，也非常罕见。</p>
<p>另据河南官媒报导，14日当天，不只是洛阳市，河南郑州和安阳市也降下冰雹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103834219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103834219" style="width: 551px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id103834219-5fab6fcfaaa2fe6ac3c6c22da59ed33d.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103834219" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id103834219-5fab6fcfaaa2fe6ac3c6c22da59ed33d.jpg" alt="" width="551" b="421" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103834219" class="wp-caption-text">12月14日，河南省多地下冰雹。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>稍早前，12月10日至11日，河南省多地遭遇暴雪袭击，伴随罕见的紫色闪电、雷鸣。</p>
<p>当时网民热议“雷打雪”的天象。有人提及俗语“雷打雪，人吃铁”，即冬季出现“雷打雪”，预示着粮食欠收，甚至会出现颗粒无收的情况，人们将无粮食可吃。</p>
<p>（责任编辑：历澄宇）</p>
