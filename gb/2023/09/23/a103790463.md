<h2>第一道~第戎芥末烧肉</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103790480" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-103790480 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103790480-1-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第戎芥末烧肉风味独特</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103790481" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103790481" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103790481-2-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第戎芥末烧肉 盛盘后再挤些柠檬汁</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103790482" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103790482" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103790482-3-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">美味的第戎芥末烧肉</figcaption></figure>
<p>食材：<br />
里肌肉180g、洋葱块30g、蒜碎10g、洋菇50g、红萝卜100g、月桂叶2片、柠檬2g、盐巴少许、黑胡椒粗碎少许、第戎芥末酱15g、奶油30g、白酒50g、鲜奶油50g。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 洋葱对切大块，红萝卜切小块后用水煮一下。柠檬对半切，里肌排切一口大小，再用胡椒、盐、面粉揉腌一下。<br />
2. 取平底锅先将肉煎至上色，备用。<br />
3. 原锅加入菇、洋葱炒香。再加些奶油炒蒜头，再加入红萝卜、第戎芥末酱、肉拌炒。<br />
4. 锅内拌炒均匀后炝入白酒，持续拌匀后加入水，开中小火炖煮约5分钟左右，放入月桂叶再大火炖煮约十分钟左右。<br />
5. 盛盘后挤些柠檬汁，最后加上可食用蔬菜装饰即可。</p>
<h2>第二道~菌菇炖饭</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103790483" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103790483" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103790483-22-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">菌菇炖饭 用已煮熟的白饭</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103790484" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103790484" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103790484-21-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">菌菇炖饭轻松在家做</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103790485" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103790485" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103790485-23-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">步骤简单的菌菇炖饭</figcaption></figure>
<p>食材：<br />
白饭180g、洋菇80g、鸿喜菇30g、美白菇30g、白酒50g、鲜奶油100g、奶油30g、洋葱碎15g、黑胡椒粉少许、盐巴少许。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 取平底锅融化奶油炒香洋葱所有菇类，加入白饭炒开再倒入水，缩汁。<br />
2. 二次加水及鲜奶油，开大火再缩汁，调味后加入奶油，饭滑顺即可装盘，上面可再加些装饰。</p>
