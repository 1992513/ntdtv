<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欧冠：切尔西主场逆转晋级 斯特林 哈弗茨建功</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664209-GettyImages-1471955239-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2023年3月7日，2022-2023年度欧冠16强淘汰赛次回合，切尔西主场2比0力克多特蒙德，从而以两回合总比分2比1逆转晋级八强，前锋斯特林和哈弗茨为球队建功。(Clive Rose/Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年03月08日讯】当地时间3月7日，2022-2023年度<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠16强淘汰赛.md#1">欧冠16强淘汰赛</a>次回合展开激战，“蓝军”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/切尔西队.md#1">切尔西队</a>主场2比0力克德甲劲旅<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/多特蒙德队.md#1">多特蒙德队</a>，从而以两回合总比分2比1逆转晋级八强，前锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/斯特林.md#1">斯特林</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/哈弗茨.md#1">哈弗茨</a>为球队建功。</p>
<p>上半场第5分钟，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/切尔西队.md#1">切尔西队</a>左路突破，边锋菲利克斯（Jo?o Félix）下底后小角度射门，被及时出击的门将封出。</p>
<p>第8分钟，切尔西队过顶球传对方后卫身后，前锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/哈弗茨.md#1">哈弗茨</a>（Kai Havertz）快速前插，突入禁区右路射门，打中边网。然而慢镜头显示，哈弗茨在射门的一瞬间，对方防守球员有个明显推人的动作，但主裁判未予判罚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103664162" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664162-GettyImages-1247869240.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103664162" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664162-GettyImages-1247869240-600x392.jpg" alt="Chelsea's German midfielder Kai Havertz (R) runs with the ball during the UEFA Champions League round of 16 second-leg football match between Chelsea and Borrusia Dortmund at Stamford Bridge in London on March 7, 2023. (Photo by Glyn KIRK / IKIMAGES / AFP) (Photo by GLYN KIRK/IKIMAGES/AFP via Getty Images)" width="600" b="392" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第8分钟，切尔西队过顶球传对方后卫身后，前锋哈弗茨快速前插，突入禁区右路射门，打中边网。(GLYN KIRK/IKIMAGES/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第17分钟，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/多特蒙德队.md#1">多特蒙德队</a>获得禁区前中路任意球，中场球员马尔科·罗伊斯（Marco Reus）直接攻门，对方门将凯帕·阿里萨巴拉加（Kepa Arrizabalaga）表现神勇，横身将球扑出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103664171" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664171-GettyImages-1247869984.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103664171" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664171-GettyImages-1247869984-600x409.jpg" alt="Chelsea's Spanish goalkeeper Kepa Arrizabalaga dices to save a free-kick during the UEFA Champions League round of 16 second-leg football match between Chelsea and Borrusia Dortmund at Stamford Bridge in London on March 7, 2023. (Photo by ADRIAN DENNIS / AFP) (Photo by ADRIAN DENNIS/AFP via Getty Images)" width="600" b="409" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第17分钟，多特蒙德队获得禁区前中路任意球，中场球员马尔科·罗伊斯直接攻门，切尔西门将凯帕·阿里萨巴拉加表现神勇，横身将球扑出。(ADRIAN DENNIS/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第28分钟，切尔西队右路传中，对方后卫解围，哈弗茨在大禁区边缘迎球劲射，击中右侧立柱弹出底线，惊出客队门将一身冷汗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103664175" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664175-GettyImages-1247870052.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103664175" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664175-GettyImages-1247870052-600x340.jpg" alt="Chelsea's German midfielder Kai Havertz (C) watches his shot hit the post during the UEFA Champions League round of 16 second-leg football match between Chelsea and Borrusia Dortmund at Stamford Bridge in London on March 7, 2023. <p>第40分钟，切尔西队前场左边路开出任意球，45度角斜传禁区引发混乱，前锋菲利克斯在点球点附近起脚射门，但角度太正，被对方门将挡出。</p>
<p>第44分钟，切尔西队左路形成突破，中场球员奇尔维尔（Ben Chilwell）下底传中，队友<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/斯特林.md#1">斯特林</a>（Raheem Sterling）第一脚射门踢空，随后晃开角度在12码处劲射破网，帮助球队打破僵局。切尔西队在近13个主场的欧冠比赛中都取得进球。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103664178" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664178-GettyImages-1471955578.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103664178" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664178-GettyImages-1471955578-600x400.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - MARCH 07: Raheem Sterling of Chelsea scores the team's first goal as Alexander Meyer of Borussia Dortmund attempts to make a save during the UEFA Champions League round of 16 leg two match between Chelsea FC and Borussia Dortmund at Stamford Bridge on March 07, 2023 in London, England. (Photo by Justin Setterfield/Getty Images)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第44分钟，切尔西队中场奇尔维尔左路下底传中，队友斯特林晃开角度在12码处劲射破门得分。(Justin Setterfield/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>上半场结束，“蓝军”暂时一球领先交换场地。</p>
<p>下半场刚开场，切尔西队左路传中时，打中对方防守球员手臂，经过VAR确认后，主裁判指向点球点。中锋哈弗茨操刀主罚，然而射门再次击中立柱弹出，这是他本场比赛第二次击中右门柱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103664181" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664181-GettyImages-1247872721.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103664181" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664181-GettyImages-1247872721-600x356.jpg" alt="Chelsea's German midfielder Kai Havertz (R) watches his penalty hit the post, before Chelsea were allowed to re-take it following a VAR review, during the UEFA Champions League round of 16 second-leg football match between Chelsea and Borrusia Dortmund at Stamford Bridge in London on March 7, 2023. (Photo by Glyn KIRK / IKIMAGES / AFP) (Photo by GLYN KIRK/IKIMAGES/AFP via Getty Images)" width="600" b="356" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第50分钟，切尔西队中锋哈弗茨主罚点球，击中右门柱弹出。(GLYN KIRK/IKIMAGES/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>而在哈弗茨罚球瞬间，多特蒙德球员提前进入大禁区，主裁判通过VAR确认后，判定重罚。哈弗茨此次一蹴而就，对方门将判断错方向，切尔西队2比0领先。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103664183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664183-GettyImages-1247871893.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103664183" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664183-GettyImages-1247871893-600x368.jpg" alt="Chelsea's German midfielder Kai Havertz (2R) celebrates scoring the team's second goal, after he was allowed to re-take his penalty, followng a VAR review, during the UEFA Champions League round of 16 second-leg football match between Chelsea and Borrusia Dortmund at Stamford Bridge in London on March 7, 2023. (Photo by Glyn KIRK / IKIMAGES / AFP) (Photo by GLYN KIRK/IKIMAGES/AFP via Getty Images)" width="600" b="368" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第53分钟，切尔西队中锋哈弗茨重罚点球，射入球门右下角。(PGLYN KIRK/IKIMAGES/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第58分钟，多特蒙德队左路传中，中锋塞巴斯蒂安·阿莱（Sebastien Haller）射门被对方防守球员封堵，贝林汉姆（Jude Bellingham）补射稍稍偏出右门柱。</p>
<p>第65分钟，多特蒙德队右边路下底倒三角回传，边后卫马里乌斯·沃尔夫（Marius Wolf）助攻至禁区内，拔脚怒射，被门将飞身扑出底线。</p>
<p>第73分钟，多特蒙德队中卫尼克拉斯·聚勒（Niklas Süle）在禁区外右侧大力远射，偏出远门柱。</p>
<p>第89分钟，多特蒙德队右路传中，贝林汉姆中路头球攻门，稍稍高出横梁。</p>
<p>最终全场比赛结束，切尔西队2比0击败多特蒙德队，以两回合2比1的总比分晋级八强。主队后卫马克·库库雷利亚因防守端出色的发挥，被评为本场比赛最佳球员。</p>

<p>切尔西首发（3-4-3）：1-凯帕·阿里萨巴拉加/32-马克·库库雷利亚，26-卡利杜·库利巴利，33-韦斯利·福法纳/21-奇尔维尔，5-恩佐（87&#8217;↑20-丹尼斯·扎卡里亚），8-科瓦西奇（83&#8217;↑10-普利西奇），24-里斯·詹姆斯/11-菲利克斯（67&#8217;↑23-加拉格尔），29-哈弗茨，17-斯特林（83&#8217;↑12-鲁本·洛夫图斯·奇克）</p>
<p>多特蒙德首发（4-1-4-1）：33-亚历山大·迈耶/13-拉斐尔·格雷罗，4-尼科·施洛特贝克，25-尼克拉斯·聚勒，17-马里乌斯·沃尔夫/23-埃姆雷·詹/11-马尔科·罗伊斯，6-沙利·厄兹詹（64&#8217;↑43-杰米·比诺·吉滕斯），22-裘德·贝林厄姆，19-尤利安·布兰特（5&#8217;↑7-乔瓦尼·雷纳）/9-塞巴斯蒂安·阿莱（77&#8217;↑21-唐尼尔·马伦）</p>
<figure id="attachment_103664157" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664157-49dea391a7f6f637dce15803e97abc86.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103664157" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103664157-49dea391a7f6f637dce15803e97abc86-600x435.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="435" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年3月7日，2022-2023年度欧洲冠军杯16强淘汰赛次回合，切尔西队主场2比0力克多特蒙德队，全场比赛技术统计。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本场比赛双方各打了半场好球，“蓝军”上半场攻势如潮，而多特蒙德队边锋阿德耶米（Karim David Adeyemi）因伤缺阵，对进攻端影响很大，缺少了一个快速反击的利器。下半场多特蒙德队加强进攻，但“蓝军”将士众志成城，守住两球胜果。</p>
<p>切尔西主教练波特（Graham Potter）取得这场关键胜利后，帅位相对稳固，而多特蒙德队则吃到了本年度的首场败仗。</p>
