<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>红烧豆腐的家常做法 简单美味又健康</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797929-950595c3ca5e61cec7e715f49e5b83e1-1-800x450.png" />
<h6>红烧豆腐的家庭做法。（作者提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>秋天天气干燥， 秋燥易伤肺。 如何润肺化痰、滋阴润燥，就是秋天养生的重点。 民间素有“金秋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/豆腐.md#1">豆腐</a>似人参”之说。豆腐具有清肺润燥、清热解毒、下气消痰、补中益气、健脾利湿、等功效。今天和大家分享<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/红烧豆腐.md#1">红烧豆腐</a>的家常做法，既简单美味又健康。</p>
<p>1.准备一块<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/豆腐.md#1">豆腐</a>改刀切成厚片，切好后放入盘中备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103797884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103797884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-medium wp-image-103797884" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797884-IMG_2520-600x450-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103797884" class="wp-caption-text">大豆腐一块。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.鸡蛋打入碗中，加入少许食盐，搅散后倒在豆腐上，让豆腐均匀的裹上蛋液。腌制5分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103797887" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103797887" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103797887" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797887-ee93e767b7b005fb6c8786ee00094da2-600x620.png" alt="" width="600" b="620" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103797887" class="wp-caption-text">把鸡蛋液淋入豆腐中。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.准备配料：大蒜拍散切成末，小葱切葱花。</p>
<p>4.调制料汁：碗中加入一勺生抽，一勺蚝油，少许的白糖提鲜，半勺老抽调色，两勺清水，少许淀粉。搅拌均匀。</p>
<p>5.起锅加少许的油。油热后下入豆腐，小火慢煎，煎至两面金黄，盛出备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103797889" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103797889" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103797889" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797889-f582b9f19d87dca66dd6d615357bec61-600x701.png" alt="" width="600" b="701" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103797889" class="wp-caption-text">豆腐煎至两面金黄。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.锅中留少许底油，油热后下入蒜末、葱炒香，倒入调好的料汁，下入豆腐，翻拌均匀，烧至2分钟左右，出锅前撒上葱花，翻炒均匀就可以出锅了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103797930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103797930" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103797930" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797930-75e33914d83a4fd250de90e7685144c4-600x663-1-600x663.png" alt="" width="600" b="663" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103797930" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/红烧豆腐.md#1">红烧豆腐</a>做好了。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这样一道美味的红烧豆腐做好了。</p>
