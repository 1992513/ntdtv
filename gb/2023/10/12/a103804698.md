<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大头菜排毒抗感冒 这样做清脆爽口不出水</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103805273-034a5655a46fa8359c63fb66737f13cb-868x488.png" />
<h6>炒大头菜的正确做法。（作者提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>冬天是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/大头菜.md#1">大头菜</a>（包菜）盛产的季节，大头菜含有维生素C、矿物质钾以及淀粉酵素等丰富营养成分。大头菜被民间视为“天然消化药材”，有效预防肠病毒、感冒等疾病，同时可以提升免疫力。今天和大家分享炒大头菜的正确方法，清脆爽口不出水。</p>
<p>1.先将最外面的叶子全部去掉，对半切开，把中间的硬芯去掉。</p>
<p>2.准备一个大盆，先把菜叶一层层掰下来，每一片叶子用手撕成小块，不是用刀去切，手撕的包菜，边缘纹理是不规则的，容易吸收料汁的味道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103805281" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103805281" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-full wp-image-103805281" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103805281-6255564c49550fcf3fbb0966.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="512" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103805281" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.?往盆里边加入多一些的清水，清洗干净，控水捞出。</p>
<p>4.准备配料：大蒜剁成蒜末，姜切成丝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103805282" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103805282" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103805282" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103805282-f8d8ef27a55b0136130e0b85.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="571" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103805282" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>干辣椒剪成小段，用来炝锅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103805283" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103805283" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103805283" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103805283-7b965f977e8171dad77e4d79.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="569" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103805283" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.调制料汁：碗中加入一勺食盐，少许白糖提鲜，少许鸡精，一勺半的生抽，半勺干淀粉，一点点的清水，搅拌化开。</p>
<p>6.起锅烧热，加少许的食用油，油温烧热，加入蒜末、干辣椒爆香，放入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/大头菜.md#1">大头菜</a>，大火翻炒半分钟，炒至六七成熟，加入调味料汁，大火爆炒，炒熟之后，关火快速地出锅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103805278" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103805278" style="width: 898px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103805278" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103805278-b13fff6b6889a5b3082c975c13bca601.png" alt="" width="898" b="652" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103805278" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这样一道，清脆爽口的炒大头菜就做好了。</p>
