<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>红薯糯米饼 软糯香甜 学会这样做太好吃了</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803961-download-1-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>红薯糯米饼。（作者提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/红薯.md#1">红薯</a>含有丰富的纤维素、蛋白质和矿物质，具有滑肠通便，健胃益气，补中和血等功效，秋冬季节多吃红薯，可降低中风的风险和提高人体免疫力。今天和大家分享<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/红薯糯米饼.md#1">红薯糯米饼</a>的做法，简单又好吃。</p>
<p>1.准备一些<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/红薯.md#1">红薯</a>洗净，上锅蒸熟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103803944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103803944" style="width: 543px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-full wp-image-103803944" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803944-download-7.jpg" alt="" width="543" b="492" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103803944" class="wp-caption-text">红薯蒸熟。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.红薯去皮后，放入大碗中压成泥，越细腻越好。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103803945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103803945" style="width: 599px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103803945" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803945-download-6.jpg" alt="" width="599" b="554" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103803945" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.红薯没有那么甜，加点糖提味，分次加入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/糯米粉.md#1">糯米粉</a>拌匀，糯米粉的用量要酌情添加，翻拌至无干面粉的面团，不粘碗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103803948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103803948" style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103803948" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803948-download-4.jpg" alt="" width="597" b="577" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103803948" class="wp-caption-text">红薯中放入糖和面粉。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103803951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103803951" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103803951" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803951-download-3.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="481" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103803951" class="wp-caption-text">红薯<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/糯米粉.md#1">糯米粉</a>面团。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.取一小块面团50克左右揉圆，捏成小窝状，放入10克的红豆沙，虎口收拢法包好，揉光滑，按成圆饼形状。这样一个生坯就完成了。</p>
<p>5.平底锅开小火预热刷油，依次放入小饼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103803952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103803952" style="width: 597px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103803952" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803952-download-2.jpg" alt="" width="597" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103803952" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.盖上盖子熟的快，底部烙至上色后翻面，两翻烙一次，烙至两面金黄色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103803955" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103803955" style="width: 595px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103803955" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803955-download.jpg" alt="" width="595" b="623" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103803955" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.小饼微微鼓起就说明熟透，就可以出锅了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103803958" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103803958" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103803958" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803958-download-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="530" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103803958" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这样<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/红薯糯米饼.md#1">红薯糯米饼</a>就做好了，软糯香甜，太好吃了。</p>
<p>（责任编辑：嘉欣）</p>
