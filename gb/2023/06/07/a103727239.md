<h2>北京首度出现<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/猴痘病例.md#1">猴痘病例</a> 2人透过亲密接触感染</h2>

<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/斯诺克1.md#1">斯诺克1</a>0人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/假球案.md#1">假球案</a> 世界台联：中国2球员终身禁赛</h2>
