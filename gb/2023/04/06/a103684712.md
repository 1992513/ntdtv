<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>小米银耳粥的家庭做法 养胃美颜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103687975-IMG_2686-800x450.jpeg" />
<h6>小米银耳粥的做法。（作者提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/小米.md#1">小米</a>有清热解渴、健胃除湿、和胃安眠等功效。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/银耳.md#1">银耳</a>含丰富的胶质，能滋润皮肤，抵御干燥。小米银耳熬粥是最补的吃法之一， 下面介绍小米银耳粥的做法。</p>
<p>1.首先<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/银耳.md#1">银耳</a>要泡发后，撕成小块，装盘备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103687968" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103687968" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103687968-IMG_2684-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">银耳（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.枸杞用清水洗净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103687971" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103687971" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103687971-IMG_2664-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">枸杞（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/小米.md#1">小米</a>200克洗净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103687969" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103687969" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103687969-IMG_2682-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小米（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.沙锅中放入清水，是小米10倍左右，加入银耳，大火煮开后转小火煮10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103687972" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103687972" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103687972-IMG_2663-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.放入小米，大火煮开后，转小火。可搅拌几次，防止糊底。</p>
<p>6.熬至30分钟后，加入泡好的枸杞和冰糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103687973" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103687973" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103687973-IMG_2692-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">冰糖（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.再小火煮10分钟，关火加盖，焖10分钟，小米银耳粥就做好了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103687974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103687974" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103687974-IMG_2687-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">小米银耳粥（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
