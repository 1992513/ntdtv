<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>传科兴疫苗引发白血病 多地删接种记录疑毁证据</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/GettyImages-1233825540-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>中国科兴控股生物技术有限公司研制的中共病毒（COVID-19）疫苗。(JUNI KRISWANTO/AFP via Getty Images)  </h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2022年06月05日讯】中国民众控诉国产中共病毒（COVID-19）疫苗导致<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/白血病.md#1">白血病</a>，据称多为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/科兴疫苗.md#1">科兴疫苗</a>。多地传出回收疫苗接种证和删除接种记录，网友怀疑当局<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/毁灭证据.md#1">毁灭证据</a>，以防被起诉追责。</p>
<p>近日来自中国30多个省的数千民众连发公开信，指控接种国产疫苗导致<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/白血病.md#1">白血病</a>。网传多数为接种<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/科兴疫苗.md#1">科兴疫苗</a>。</p>
<p>辽宁省盘锦市盘山县一名居民也告诉新唐人，发病者以打科兴疫苗的居多，他手上有一个900多人的名单，“基本都是科兴”。他说，“现在的问题是没人给你鉴定，没人敢鉴定。”</p>
<p>与此同时，多地网民传出疑似官方开始毁灭疫苗接种的消息。稍早传出的消息是，有的地方学校开始收回学生的疫苗接种证。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103446981" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/001.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103446981" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/001-600x1299.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="866" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">网络图片。</figcaption></figure>
<p>随后多地陆续传出支付宝、健康宝等App中的个人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/疫苗接种记录.md#1">疫苗接种记录</a>突然消失。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/003.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-103446984" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/003-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="711" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/004.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103446985 aligncenter" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/004-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="711" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103446997" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/FUKcHUUWIAEqeeT.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103446997" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/FUKcHUUWIAEqeeT-600x1146.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="764" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">网络图片。</figcaption></figure>
<p>有的地方据传只是孩子的接种记录消失，有的地方则是全面删除接种记录。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/002.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-103446986" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/002-600x653.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="435" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103446992" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/005-2.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103446992" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/005-2-600x1299.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="866" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">网络图片。</figcaption></figure>
<p>也有消息称，有地方的中小学和幼儿园突然让家长填表，排查孩子接种的是哪个品牌的疫苗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103446989" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/006.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103446989" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/006-600x1299.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="866" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">网络图片。</figcaption></figure>
<p>有网民纷纷猜测，这是否“科兴要出事”的预兆。</p>
<p>但事实上，引发严重副作用的国产疫苗并不止科兴一家。自从中共强推国产疫苗开始，各地就接连传出打疫苗致死的消息，但官方一概将其归结为“耦合症”，并对试图维权的死者家人进行打压。</p>
<p>除了白血病，近日还有众多网民集体控诉接种国产疫苗导致糖尿病。还有很多网民指控国产疫苗引发中风、心肌炎、癌症等众多恶性疾病。</p>
<p>网民控诉疫苗导致白血病的公开信已被当局封杀。但相关舆论继续发酵。有人认为当局会继续打压民众维权。也有人猜测，中共是否会推出一两个替罪羊来平息民愤。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/FUVUXLSUcActxYf.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-103447002" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/FUVUXLSUcActxYf-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103447003" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/FUVUXLQUcAAOBHB.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103447003" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/06/FUVUXLQUcAAOBHB-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="300" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">网络图片。</figcaption></figure>
